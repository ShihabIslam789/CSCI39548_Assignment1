Here is a type of Lion.



These animals usually live in grasslands, scrubs, or open woodland areas. Usually they can be found in the sub-Saharan African region.
Unlike cats, who don't stay in groups, Lions perfer to stay in groups called Prides. They stick together usually when its time to hunt or share meals.
The group could contain as little as 4 members or as big as 37 members. The lions usually eat on animals varying in sizes.
They can eat rodents, baboons, buffalo, or even hippopotamuses. But they get medium sized creatures more often usually.
